# Artificial Intelligence in BioMedical Applications
**Course Code:** BCSE303L  
**Department:** BTECH BIO TECH  
**Total Duration:** 45 hours  
**Course Description:** This course focuses on the practical application of artificial intelligence techniques in biomedical fields, emphasizing advanced algorithms and methodologies. The course is designed to be progressive, building from foundational concepts to more complex applications.

## Course Objectives
1. Understand the fundamental principles of artificial intelligence and their application in biomedical research.
2. Implement machine learning models for predictive analytics in healthcare.
3. Apply deep learning techniques to analyze biomedical data.
4. Design and develop AI systems for personalized medicine.
5. Evaluate the ethical and legal implications of AI in biomedical applications.

## Course Outcomes
1. Understand and apply AI techniques to solve biomedical problems.
2. Develop and implement machine learning models for healthcare applications.
3. Analyze and interpret biomedical data using advanced AI methods.
4. Design and evaluate AI systems for personalized medicine.
5. Assess the ethical and legal considerations of AI in biomedical applications.

## Module-wise Syllabus

---

### **### Module 1: ** *Introduction to Artificial Intelligence and Biomedical Applications (6 hours)*

- **Overview:** Introduction to AI, its role in biomedical applications, and the importance of AI in healthcare.
- **Topics:**
  - Definition and history of AI.
  - Types of AI: supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement learning.
  - AI in healthcare: current and future applications.
  - Overview of biomedical data types and sources.

---

### **### Module 2: ** *Machine Learning Fundamentals (8 hours)*

- **Overview:** Introduction to machine learning, key algorithms, and their applications in biomedical research.
- **Topics:**
  - Supervised learning: regression, classification, support vector machines.
  - Unsupervised learning: clustering, dimensionality reduction.
  - Ensemble methods: random forests, gradient boosting.
  - Practical implementation using Python and scikit-learn.

---

### **### Module 3: ** *Deep Learning in Biomedical Applications (10 hours)*

- **Overview:** Introduction to deep learning, neural networks, and their applications in biomedical data analysis.
- **Topics:**
  - Neural networks: architecture, activation functions, backpropagation.
  - Convolutional neural networks (CNNs): image classification, medical imaging.
  - Recurrent neural networks (RNNs): sequence modeling, time series analysis.
  - Generative adversarial networks (GANs): data generation and augmentation.

---

### **### Module 4: ** *Personalized Medicine and AI (10 hours)*

- **Overview:** Application of AI in personalized medicine, including genomics and precision healthcare.
- **Topics:**
  - Genomics and AI: variant calling, gene expression analysis.
  - Precision medicine: drug discovery, treatment prediction.
  - Electronic health records (EHRs): data extraction and analysis.
  - Case studies: AI in personalized cancer treatment.

---

### **### Module 5: ** *Ethical and Legal Considerations (11 hours)*

- **Overview:** Ethical and legal issues in AI, including data privacy, bias, and accountability.
- **Topics:**
  - Data privacy and security: HIPAA, GDPR.
  - Bias in AI: fairness, transparency.
  - Accountability and liability: legal frameworks.
  - Case studies: AI in healthcare and ethical dilemmas.

## Case Studies
1. **Google Health:** Utilizes AI to improve the accuracy of breast cancer screening.
2. **IBM Watson Health:** Employs AI for personalized treatment plans and disease diagnosis.
3. **Pfizer:** Uses AI to accelerate drug discovery and development.
4. **Medtronic:** Applies AI to improve patient monitoring and treatment outcomes.
